This lab is where I turn ideas into working tools

I started building web tools as a way to solve my own everyday problems — tracking debts, automating repetitive work, organizing information. What began as personal projects quickly became something I wanted to share with others.

Every app in this lab is free to use, runs entirely in your browser, and respects your privacy. No accounts needed, no data sent anywhere. Just practical tools that work.

I'm not a traditional developer — I'm someone who loves finding smart solutions and turning them into reality with the help of modern tools. If you have an idea for a useful app, I'd love to hear about it!
